Our Journey

From a small logistics startup to a global industry leader, explore the milestones that shaped TradeFlow's success.

2010

Company Foundation

TradeFlow was founded with a vision to revolutionize global logistics through technology and innovation. Started operations with a focus on Asia-Pacific trade lanes and container shipping services.

2013

Digital Transformation

Launched our first digital platform for real-time tracking and documentation. Introduced API integrations and automated booking systems, setting new industry standards.

2016

Global Expansion

Expanded operations to Europe and Americas, establishing partnerships with major carriers. Opened regional offices in Rotterdam, Los Angeles, and Dubai to serve global clients.

2019

AI Integration

Implemented artificial intelligence for route optimization and predictive analytics. Achieved 25% improvement in delivery times and significant cost reductions for clients.

2022

Sustainability Initiative

Launched green logistics program with carbon-neutral shipping options and sustainable warehousing. Partnered with environmental organizations to offset emissions and promote eco-friendly practices.

2025

Industry Leadership

Recognized as a top 10 global logistics provider with 3.2M TEU capacity and operations in 130+ countries. Leading the industry in digital innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
